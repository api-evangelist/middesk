Not all SMB underwriting tools perform the same in production, and this piece outlines four critical categories to evaluate when designing an underwriting system. It argues successful lenders consolidate business identity verification, lien and bankruptcy data, person-level risk checks, and unified workflows into a single platform rather than managing multiple disconnected vendors.
